About Fun-Doo

The vision at "Illuminer"is to create products & solutions that can herald a change in how we see, do, learn, experience & live!

In 2020 the founders of Illuminer, like everyone else, were caught in the mayhem of Covid lock-down. It gave them a chance to observe first-hand and experience the kids' world turning upside down with schools going online, social life reducing to nil and and the "add-on" classes, activities & kits filling in the time slots that were meant for play time.

From that emerged a clear vision for Fun-Doo:
"To bring a unique product to enable learning via immersive mediums, in order to nurture the kid’s natural curiosity and develop specific skill-sets along the journey. The goal is to create a long-term sustainable model oriented pedagogic solution for driving learning outcomes for children."

At Fun-Doo, we firmly believe that...
* The differentiator for success in the new world will be solid core values, a strong character & mental strength; seeds of which must be sown at a young age.
* There is so much knowledge & talent that doesn’t find the right outlet channel
